570 CE

Born approximately 570 CE (Year of the Elephant) in the Arabian city of Mecca, Muhammad was orphaned at an early age; he was raised under the care of his paternal uncle Abu Talib.

607 CE

at age 40, he reported being visited by Gabriel in the cave,where he stated he received his first revelation from God

610 CE

Muhammad started preaching these revelations publicly,proclaiming that "God is One", that complete "surrender" (islam) to him is the right course of action (din),and that he was a prophet and messenger of God, similar to the other prophets in Islam.

622 CE

he and his followers migrated from Mecca to Medina (then known as Yathrib) in the year 622. This event, the Hijra, marks the beginning of the Islamic calendar, also known as the Hijri Calendar.

632 CE

a few months after returning from the Farewell Pilgrimage, he fell ill and died. Before his death, most of the Arabian Peninsula had converted to Islam.
